MonsterCraft 2 - making sequels is hard

After good reception of MonsterCraft it was obvious that we should release a sequel. Turns out, making a good sequel is not as easy as it seems - we had to add new stuff without overcomplicating things.

Here is a list of things we decided to do in the sequel:

  • Added in a third race - demons. Demons can't be crafted, but player can capture and use them later in the game.
  • Added an ability to buy armor for your units. So now each unit has 3 tiers: no armor, light armor, heavy armor.
  • Added spells - a way for a player to take part in fights.
  • Made a game longer by adding more worlds\levels.
  • Added a boss in the end of each world. Final boss also tricky and required smart use of spells to win.
  • Added achievements.
