    Animated Banners - a first hobby job

    Somwhere in 2011 (on my first year of university) I started earning some pocket money as a freelancer. It wasn't much, but it was fun. I always wanted to become an animator - and this job was basically about animating adverisement banners in Adobe Flash.

    Here are some examples of those banners. For more you can look into my fl.ru profile.

     

    The more I used Adobe Flash, the more creative I started getting. I found out that you can also do interactive stuff in flash, so I dived into simple scripting and created this: a plate-clock that shows real time, with a silly secret that you could find if you drag a knife or a fork with your mouse!

    This was an incredible experience and helped me learn basics of animation and scripting, which I later used to develop flash games.
