Monster Craft - A good idea is worth a lot

So I was hanging out with my best friend and were brainstoring game ideas. We weren't planning to develop one together - we just enjoyed discussing interesting game ideas.

That day we came up with the idea of MonsterCraft:

  • Take a basic combination logic: A+B=C and use it to craft units. Make the result logical: robot+robot = bigger robot, zombie+zombie = big zombie, robot+zombie = cyborg zombie!

I described the idea to the team I made Cortex Fortress with and we developed the game fairly quickly.

It was quite successful: we got a lot of good reviews and people started making walthroughs and letsplays of MonsterCraft on youtube. One of those videos got 7 million views:

Later we started to notice reskinned versions of our game on the web and android versions on Google Play Store:

We contacted Google to take those down. Eventually they did take down some of those, but new ones kept popping up and we gave up. I'd say proudly I accepted this as a sign of success and popularity.

If you want, you play MonsterCraft online on ArmorGames.
