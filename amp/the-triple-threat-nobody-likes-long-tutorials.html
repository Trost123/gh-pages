You could use the helicopter to move a unit from one tile to another, you had a storage slot if you wanted to save some unit for later use, you had dynamite to blow up zombies and get money for that.

The game also featured 10 missions, completing which awarded you extra money, which you could spend in shop:

In fact, I opened the game to make a few screenshots for this text and spent an hour playing because it's quite addictive.
