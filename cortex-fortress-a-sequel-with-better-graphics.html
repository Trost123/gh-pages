Cortex Fortress - Developing together

After releasing my first game - BrainDef, I got my first fan!

It was not just an ordinary fan, it was a guy, experienced in making 2d and 3d art for games. He offered to join me and make another game for a 50\50 profits split when it goes live. I was so hyped back then! Later on he also invited his friend into our little team, who took care of SFX and music.

Those people helped me gain confidence and motivation to release my games faster and brought the quality level of my games way up.
